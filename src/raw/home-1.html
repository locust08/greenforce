About Green Force Sustainability

Established in 2010. Green Force Sustainability is a leading metal recycling company specialise in ferrous metal recycling. Our operations are supported by our wholly owned collecting and processing facilities and an extensive network of recyclable ferrous metals suppliers / dealers across Malaysia from which we source recyclable materials and supply to steel mills.
