Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 27/02/2025

Loanly ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services, visit our website, or interact with us in any way.

By accessing or using our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue the use of our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information:
  • Name, address, email, phone number, and other identifying details provided by you.
  • Financial information such as income, credit score, and loan preferences necessary to provide our services.
Non-Personal Information:
  • IP addresses, browser types, and device details collected automatically to improve website functionality.
  • Cookies and tracking technologies that enhance your user experience on our site.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To process loan applications and provide financial services tailored to your needs.
  • To communicate with you about your account, updates, and promotions.
  • To ensure compliance with legal and regulatory obligations.
  • To improve our services and personalize your experience.
  • To prevent fraudulent activities and protect your data.

3. Sharing of Information

Loanly does not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with:

  • Service providers and partners who assist in delivering our services.
  • Regulatory authorities or law enforcement agencies when required by law.
  • Credit bureaus and financial institutions as part of loan processing and evaluation.

All third parties receiving your information are obligated to maintain confidentiality and use your data solely for the intended purpose.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Despite our best efforts, no data transmission or storage system can be guaranteed as 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

  • Access and Update: You may request to review or update your personal information by contacting us directly.
  • Marketing Preferences: You can opt out of receiving marketing communications by following the instructions in our emails or contacting us.
  • Cookies: You may modify your browser settings to reject cookies; however, this may limit some website functionality.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may include links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before sharing any information.

7. Children's Privacy

Loanly's services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the revised policy on our website and updating the "Last Updated" date above.
