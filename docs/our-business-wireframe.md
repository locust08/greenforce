# Our Business Page Wireframe

## Goal

Create a modern, corporate-industrial Our Business page for Green Force that explains the Group's two core business areas: ferrous scrap metal recycling and finished steel product trading. The page should help visitors understand what the company does, where it operates, and how the business connects to product/service enquiries.

## Source Content

- Page heading: Our Business
- Group overview: The Group comprises Green Force Sustainability, Wise Metal Recycling and Strader.
- Business scope: Metal recycling operations and finished steel products trading.
- Recycling companies: Green Force Sustainability and Wise Metal Recycling.
- Recycling focus: Ferrous scrap metal recycling.
- Recycling description: Sourcing and processing scrap metal from businesses and recyclers to supply key steel mills and industrial customers in Malaysia according to their specifications.
- Steel trading company: Strader.
- Steel trading focus: Finished steel product trading.
- Steel trading description: Trading finished steel products across Malaysia and delivering high quality steel solutions for diverse customers and industry partners.
- Operations intro: Ferrous metal recycling operations are supported by wholly owned collection and processing facilities and an extensive supplier network across Malaysia.
- Operational sites: Kempas, Bukit Kemuning, Kajang, Kuantan, Senawang.
- Products: Steel Billets, Steel Coils, Round Bars, Angle Bars, Rebars.
- Visual requirement: professional, high-resolution images relevant to recycling and steel industry.
- Brand direction: modern corporate-industrial identity using orange, silver, red, and white.

## Implementation Plan

1. Keep the existing `our-business.astro` page shell and `WebflowPage` transformation pattern.
2. Replace the current loan-program template import/content with a Green Force business layout.
3. Build the page into seven clear sections: hero, group overview, recycling arm, operational sites, steel trading arm, products snapshot, and CTA band.
4. Use existing industrial assets first: `/images/contact-hero-industrial.png`, `/images/greenforce-announcement-facility.webp`, `/images/greenforce-announcement-industrial.webp`, and `/images/point3d-commercial-imaging-ltd-Rb3HbkNNoLQ-unsplash.jpg`.
5. Use operational site cards with location actions for Google Maps and Waze.
6. Keep product details light on this page and link deeper detail to `/product-services`.
7. Use orange for CTAs and section accents, silver/white for surfaces, charcoal for high-contrast industrial bands, and restrained red for small emphasis only.
8. Avoid investor relations content; the source says it should remain hidden/inactive.
9. Add responsive CSS so two-column business sections stack cleanly on mobile and site cards become single-column.
10. Verify with `npm run build`, then run the page locally and inspect `/our-business` in-browser.

## Visual Direction

Hero image:

```text
Wide website hero photograph of a modern metal recycling and steel processing
operation in Malaysia, organized scrap collection yard, stacked steel materials,
clean industrial facility, realistic daylight, corporate and professional,
no text, no logos, 16:9 composition with left-side negative space for headline.
```

Section styling:

- Hero: full-width image with dark overlay, white title text, and a small orange accent.
- Group overview: clean white section with three company cards.
- Recycling arm: image/text split with facility imagery and practical business copy.
- Operational sites: light silver background, dense but readable site cards with map actions.
- Steel trading arm: reversed image/text split with stock/product imagery.
- Products snapshot: simple icon or text tiles, not a full product catalog.
- CTA band: charcoal or deep industrial image background with orange CTA.
